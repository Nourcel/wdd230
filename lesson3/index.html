Site Purpose

The Cape Town chamber of commerce has a purpose of helping local and internation businesses grow by looking into their interests and their concerns, running work-shops and arranging trainning, in order to upskill member's knowledge about compliance and regulatory governance, digitalization, marketing and tax education, giving businesses an opportunity to promote their products or services to other businesses by creating potential business partners.
